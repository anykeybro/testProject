United States Census

On April 1, 2000, the Bureau of the Census in the United States attempted to count every U.S. citizen and every resident. When counting such a large number of people, however, mistakes of various sorts are likely to arise. In 2000, many people were employed by the Bureau of the Census who were, in essence, trying to verify some of the information obtained by other census workers.
